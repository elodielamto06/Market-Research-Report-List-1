<p><ul><li>Series Connection</li><li>Parallel Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Two Stage Pump market can be segmented into Series Connection and Parallel Connection types. In Series Connection, pumps are arranged sequentially to increase pressure, suitable for applications needing higher discharge pressures, such as high-rise buildings or industrial processes. Conversely, Parallel Connection involves multiple pumps working simultaneously to enhance flow capacity, ideal for systems requiring increased flow rates and redundancy, such as irrigation systems or water supply networks. These configurations allow users to select optimal solutions based on efficiency and application needs.</p></p>
