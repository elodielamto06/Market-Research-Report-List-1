<p><p>The Microwave Ablators market is primarily categorized into two types: Microwave Thermotherapy Ablators and Cold Cycle Ablators. Microwave Thermotherapy Ablators utilize high-frequency microwave energy to heat and destroy targeted tissues, effectively treating tumors and other ailments. In contrast, Cold Cycle Ablators rely on cryogenic technology to freeze and ablate tissue, offering a different therapeutic approach. Both methods are gaining traction in minimally invasive procedures, reflecting advancements in medical technology and improving patient outcomes in various clinical applications.</p></p>
