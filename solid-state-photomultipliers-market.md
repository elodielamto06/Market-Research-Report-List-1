<p><p>The Solid-State Photomultipliers (SSPM) market is categorized into two main types: Monocoque Type and Array Type. Monocoque Type SSPMs feature a single, unified construction that optimizes compactness and ease of integration, making them suitable for applications requiring high sensitivity in confined spaces. In contrast, Array Type SSPMs consist of multiple independent detectors arranged in a grid, enhancing their ability to capture spatial information and improving overall light detection efficiency, ideal for larger-scale applications in scientific and industrial fields.</p></p>
