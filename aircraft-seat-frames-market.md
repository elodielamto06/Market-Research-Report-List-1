<p><p>The aircraft seat frames market is classified into three primary types: aluminium, magnesium, and composites. Aluminium frames are favored for their strength-to-weight ratio and durability, making them a traditional choice in aviation. Magnesium frames are lighter than aluminium, offering weight reduction benefits, but their use is limited by corrosion concerns. Composites, which include materials like carbon fiber, provide enhanced strength while being lighter and allowing for complex designs, catering to advancements in fuel efficiency and passenger comfort.</p></p>
