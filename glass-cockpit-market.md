<p><p>The Glass Cockpit market comprises several key types. The Primary Flight Display (PFD) presents essential flight data, such as altitude and speed, in a streamlined format. The Multi-Function Display (MFD) integrates various information, including navigation and weather data, enhancing situational awareness. Backup Displays offer redundancy for critical flight information, ensuring safety during emergencies. Mission Displays, often used in military aviation, provide specialized data related to mission parameters, enhancing operational effectiveness. Together, these displays enhance cockpit efficiency and pilot decision-making.</p></p>
