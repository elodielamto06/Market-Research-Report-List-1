<p><p>The home solar power system market includes three main types: pitched roof systems, flat roof systems, and ground-mounted systems. Pitched roof systems are designed for slanted roofs, maximizing sunlight capture. Flat roof systems utilize horizontal surfaces, requiring specialized mounting solutions for optimal angle and efficiency. Ground-mounted systems, installed directly on the ground, offer flexibility in positioning and can accommodate larger arrays. Each type caters to different residential setups and preferences, influencing market demand and installation choices accordingly.</p></p>
