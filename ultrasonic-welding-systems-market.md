<p><p>The ultrasonic welding systems market is divided into two main types: plastic ultrasonic welders and metal ultrasonic welders. Plastic ultrasonic welders utilize high-frequency vibrations to create heat at the interface of plastic parts, enabling them to bond quickly and securely. This technology is widely used in automotive, packaging, and electronics industries. Metal ultrasonic welders, on the other hand, employ similar principles to join metal components, ensuring strong and durable connections. These systems are increasingly used in aerospace and manufacturing sectors for precision welding applications.</p></p>
