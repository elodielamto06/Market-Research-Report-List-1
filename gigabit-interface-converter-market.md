<p><p>The Gigabit Interface Converter (GBIC) market is segmented into Multi-Mode GBIC and Single-Mode GBIC types. Multi-Mode GBICs are designed for short-distance data transmission over multimode fiber, ideal for local area networks and data centers. In contrast, Single-Mode GBICs are optimized for long-distance communication using single-mode fiber, offering higher bandwidth and longer reach, making them suitable for wide area networks. Each type caters to specific networking needs based on distance, bandwidth, and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
