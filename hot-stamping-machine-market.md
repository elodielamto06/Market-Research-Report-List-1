<p><strong>The Hot Stamping Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat-Flat</li><li>Round-Flat</li><li>Round-Round</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot stamping machine market is categorized into three main types: Flat-Flat, Round-Flat, and Round-Round. Flat-Flat machines utilize flat surfaces for both the die and substrate, making them suitable for printing on flat items. Round-Flat machines feature a round die and flat substrate, ideal for cylindrical objects such as bottles. Round-Round machines employ both round dies and substrates, primarily used for items like pens or caps, enhancing flexibility across various product shapes and material types.</p></p>
