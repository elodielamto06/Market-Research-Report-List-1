Respiratory measurements, including spirometry and pulse oximetry, evaluate airflow and oxygen levels, crucial for diseases like asthma and COPD. Blood gas tests analyze oxygen and carbon dioxide levels in the blood, providing insights into respiratory function. Other market segments include molecular tests and biopsies, enhancing diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
