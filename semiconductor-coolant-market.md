<p><p>The semiconductor coolant market features various types, prominently including per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) and perfluoropolyethers (PFPE). PFAS are synthetic chemicals known for their thermal stability and low surface tension, making them effective for high-performance cooling applications. PFPE, on the other hand, offers superior thermal conductivity and is chemically inert, ideal for environments requiring non-reactive cooling agents. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and reliability of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
