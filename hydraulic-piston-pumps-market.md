<p><p>Hydraulic piston pumps are essential in various industrial applications, categorized into two main types based on displacement: multiple displacements and single displacement. </p><p>Multiple displacement pumps offer variable output by allowing adjustments to the piston stroke length, making them versatile for varying operational demands. In contrast, single displacement pumps deliver a constant flow rate, ideal for applications requiring steady pressure without fluctuation. Both types play crucial roles in hydraulic systems, impacting efficiency, control, and application suitability across sectors such as manufacturing and construction.</p></p>
