<p><p>Continuous casting machines are essential in metallurgy for producing metal shapes. In the market, two prominent types are the curvilinear and radial types. Curvilinear machines feature a curved path for molten metal, allowing for better control and uniform cooling, which enhances product quality. Radial machines, on the other hand, utilize a rotating mold system that efficiently shapes and solidifies metal in a radial manner, optimizing space and production speed. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs in the metals industry.</p></p>
