<p><p>The Eye Allergy Therapeutics Market encompasses several types of treatments for allergic conjunctivitis and other eye allergies. Antihistamines are commonly used to alleviate symptoms like itching and redness by blocking histamine release. Corticosteroids reduce inflammation and are prescribed for more severe allergic reactions, but may have side effects with long-term use. Immunomodulators help regulate the immune response to allergens, offering a non-steroidal alternative for managing chronic allergic conditions, thus enhancing patient outcomes in eye allergy management.</p></p>
