<p><p>The switch cabinet market is segmented into three main types based on voltage levels: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage switch cabinets. Low voltage switch cabinets manage electrical systems under 1,000 volts and are commonly used in residential and commercial settings. Medium voltage cabinets operate between 1kV and 35kV, typically found in industrial applications or infrastructure. High voltage switch cabinets handle voltages above 35kV, essential for transmission and distribution systems in power generation, ensuring efficient and safe electrical management at various voltage levels.</p></p>
