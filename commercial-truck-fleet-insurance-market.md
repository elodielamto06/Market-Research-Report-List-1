<p><p>The commercial truck fleet insurance market comprises two primary types: compulsory and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is mandated by law, ensuring that fleet operators have minimum coverage for liability, protecting against third-party claims. This is essential for legal compliance and risk management. Conversely, optional insurance offers additional coverage choices, such as cargo protection and physical damage, allowing fleet operators to customize their policies based on specific needs and risks, enhancing their overall security and operational resilience.</p></p>
