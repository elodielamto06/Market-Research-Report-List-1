<p><p>The document scanner market is segmented based on sensor technology, primarily into three types: CCD (Charge Coupled Device), CIS (Contact Image Sensor), and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD scanners provide high-quality images and are often favored for professional applications requiring superior color accuracy. CIS scanners are compact, lightweight, and suitable for portable devices, making them popular in office environments. CMOS scanners offer lower power consumption and faster scanning speeds, appealing to budget-conscious users and those needing efficiency.</p></p>
