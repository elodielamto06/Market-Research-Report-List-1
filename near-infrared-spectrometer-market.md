<p><p>The Near Infrared Spectrometer market encompasses several types, including FT-NIR, UV-Vis-NIR, Raman, and others. FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near Infrared) is ideal for chemical analysis, offering high-resolution results. UV-Vis-NIR combines ultraviolet and visible light for broader spectral analysis. Raman spectroscopy uses inelastic scattering of light to provide molecular information. Other techniques may include microwave or multispectral methods. Each type serves various applications across industries like pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and food safety, driven by the need for precise chemical and material analysis.</p></p>
