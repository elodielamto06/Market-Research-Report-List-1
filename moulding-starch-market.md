<p><strong>The Moulding Starch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapidly Digestible Starch</li><li>Slowly Digestible Starch</li><li>Resistant Starch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The moulding starch market is categorized based on digestive properties. Rapidly Digestible Starch (RDS) is quickly broken down in the digestive system, providing immediate energy. Slowly Digestible Starch (SDS) digests at a slower rate, offering sustained energy release, which helps in blood sugar management. Resistant Starch (RS) is not digested in the small intestine and acts similarly to dietary fiber, promoting gut health and aiding in weight management. Each type serves distinct applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and other industries.</p></p>
