<p><p>Si/C composites, or silicon/carbon composites, are advanced materials primarily utilized in battery technology, particularly in lithium-ion batteries. Companies are increasingly focusing on research and development to optimize the silicon content in composites while managing the challenges of swelling and stability to extend battery lifespan. Furthermore, as environmental regulations tighten and consumer preferences shift toward greener alternatives, the market is poised for robust growth, attracting significant interest from major players in the automotive and electronics sectors.
