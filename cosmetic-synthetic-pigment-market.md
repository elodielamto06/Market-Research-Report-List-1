<p><p>The cosmetic synthetic pigment market encompasses two primary types: organic synthetic pigments and inorganic synthetic pigments. Organic synthetic pigments are carbon-based compounds known for their vibrant colors and excellent stability, making them popular in cosmetics for shades like reds and yellows. In contrast, inorganic synthetic pigments are mineral-based, often providing more muted tones with durability and UV resistance. Both types are crucial in formulating makeup products, providing color while ensuring safety and adherence to cosmetic regulations.</p></p>
