<p><p>The Automatic Hematology Analyzer market is categorized into 3-part and 5-part analyzers based on their capability to differentiate blood cell types. </p><p>3-part analyzers typically classify white blood cells into three categories: lymphocytes, monocytes, and granulocytes, providing essential hematological data. In contrast, 5-part analyzers offer a more detailed breakdown, categorizing white blood cells into five distinct types, which allows for more comprehensive analysis and diagnostics. This differentiation enhances clinical decision-making and improves patient care in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
