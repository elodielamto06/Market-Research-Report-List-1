<p><ul><li>Desk-Mounted Office Dividers</li><li>Floor-Standing Office Dividers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The office dividers market primarily consists of two types: desk-mounted and floor-standing dividers. Desk-mounted office dividers are attached directly to desks, offering privacy and sound absorption for individual workspaces without occupying additional floor space. Conversely, floor-standing office dividers are independent structures that create larger partitions, ideal for delineating open office layouts and enhancing collaboration areas while providing more extensive sound insulation. Both types cater to evolving workspace designs and the need for flexibility in modern office environments.</p></p>
