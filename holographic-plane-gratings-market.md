Conversely, reflective gratings reflect light and are typically utilized in scenarios where high efficiency and power handling are essential, such as in lasers and telecommunications. Both types are crucial for various applications, including spectroscopy, telecommunications, and imaging systems in scientific and industrial settings.</p></p>
