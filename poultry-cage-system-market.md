<p><p>The poultry cage system market is divided into semi-automatic and fully automatic types. In contrast, fully automatic systems are designed to operate independently, with advanced technology for feeding, watering, and managing bird health, minimizing human labor. Both systems aim to optimize productivity, animal welfare, and resource efficiency in poultry farming, catering to different operational scales and management preferences.</p></p>
