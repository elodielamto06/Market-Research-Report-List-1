<p><p>The stainless steel cabinet market is primarily segmented into two main types: 201 and 304 stainless steel. Type 201 is cost-effective and offers good corrosion resistance, making it suitable for indoor applications. In contrast, Type 304 provides superior corrosion resistance and durability, making it ideal for environments exposed to moisture, such as kitchens or laboratories. Each type caters to different customer needs, with 201 being popular in budget-sensitive projects, while 304 is preferred for high-quality and long-lasting installations.</p></p>
