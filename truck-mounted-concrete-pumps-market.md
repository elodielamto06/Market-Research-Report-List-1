<p><p>The truck-mounted concrete pumps market encompasses two primary applications: line pumps and boom pumps. Line pumps are designed for delivering concrete through pipelines, making them ideal for smaller projects requiring precision and accessibility. In contrast, boom pumps feature a robotic arm that can extend and maneuver, allowing for quick and efficient placement of concrete at varying heights, often used in larger construction sites. Both types enhance productivity, reduce labor costs, and provide versatility in concrete placement across diverse project scales.</p></p>
