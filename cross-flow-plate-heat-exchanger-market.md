<p><p>A Cross-flow Plate Heat Exchanger is a type of heat exchanger where two fluids flow at right angles to each other, facilitating efficient heat transfer between them. Typically constructed from thin, corrugated plates, this design enhances the surface area for heat exchange while minimizing the fluid volume required. These systems are widely used in various industries, including HVAC, chemical processing, food and beverage, and power generation, due to their compact size and high thermal efficiency.</p><p>The Cross-flow Plate Heat Exchanger Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing energy efficiency demands and industrial automation.
