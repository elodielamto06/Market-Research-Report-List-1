<p><p>The GaN transistor market is primarily divided into two types: GaN on SiC and GaN on Si. GaN on SiC utilizes silicon carbide substrates, offering high thermal conductivity and efficiency, making it ideal for high-power applications like RF amplifiers and power converters. In contrast, GaN on Si uses standard silicon substrates, making it more cost-effective and suitable for low to medium power applications, including consumer electronics and power supplies. Both types enhance performance, efficiency, and miniaturization in their respective markets.</p></p>
