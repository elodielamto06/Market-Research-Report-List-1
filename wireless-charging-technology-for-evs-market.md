<p><p>Wireless charging technology for electric vehicles (EVs) primarily includes three market types: Electromagnetic Induction, Magnetic Resonance, and Magneto-Dynamic Coupling. Electromagnetic Induction uses coils to transfer energy between a transmitter and receiver, while Magnetic Resonance enables more flexible alignment between charging pads, enhancing efficiency. Magneto-Dynamic Coupling utilizes oscillating magnetic fields to achieve energy transfer, allowing for larger gaps and increased freedom of movement. Each method aims to improve convenience and efficiency in charging electric vehicles wirelessly.</p></p>
