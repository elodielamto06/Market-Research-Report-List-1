<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nonlinear Optical Fiber Laser market encompasses two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a constant output of laser light, ideal for applications requiring steady, uninterrupted energy, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, Pulsed fiber lasers deliver energy in short, intense bursts, making them suitable for precision applications like micro-processing and medical uses. Both types leverage nonlinear optical effects for enhanced performance in diverse industrial and research applications.</p></p>
