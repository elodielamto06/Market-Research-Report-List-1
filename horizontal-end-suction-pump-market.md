<p><strong>The Horizontal End Suction Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Mounted End Suction Pump</li><li>Close Coupled End Suction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal end suction pump market includes two primary types: frame mounted end suction pumps and close coupled end suction pumps. Frame mounted end suction pumps are designed with a separate motor and pump base, allowing for easier maintenance and higher performance flexibility. In contrast, close coupled end suction pumps feature a compact design with the motor directly mounted to the pump, resulting in reduced footprint and lower installation costs. Both types are widely used in various industrial applications for their efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
