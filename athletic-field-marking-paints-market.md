<p><p>Athletic field marking paints are categorized into several types based on their formulation and intended use. Thermoplastic marking paint is durable and heat-resistant, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. Water-based marking paint is environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and quick-drying, suitable for temporary markings. Solvent-based marking paint offers excellent adhesion and longevity, ideal for various surfaces. Two-component road marking paint combines a base and hardener, enhancing durability and a longer lifespan, often used in demanding athletic environments.</p></p>
