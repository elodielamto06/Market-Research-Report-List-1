<p><ul><li>Positive Fluid Displacement</li><li>Negative Fluid Displacement</li><li>Neutral Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IV Connector Without Needle market encompasses three primary types of fluid displacement mechanisms: positive, negative, and neutral. Positive fluid displacement systems actively push fluid through the connector, minimizing backflow. Negative fluid displacement systems create a vacuum effect that draws fluid into the system, enhancing safety by reducing contamination risk. Neutral displacement systems maintain equilibrium, allowing fluid exchange without significant pressure changes, thus minimizing the risk of air entry and ensuring steady flow. Together, these systems enhance patient safety and efficiency in medical settings.</p></p>
