<p><p>The electric switchboard market is categorized into three types based on voltage levels: low-voltage, medium-voltage, and high-voltage. Low-voltage switchboards handle up to 1,000 volts and are commonly used in residential and commercial applications for power distribution. Medium-voltage switchboards, ranging from 1 kV to 35 kV, serve industrial and utility sectors for efficient energy management. High-voltage switchboards operate above 35 kV, primarily utilized in large-scale power generation and transmission systems, ensuring reliable and safe electrical distribution for extensive infrastructures.</p></p>
