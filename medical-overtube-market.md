<p><ul><li>Spiral Overtube</li><li>Balloon Overtube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical overtube market comprises two primary types: spiral overtubes and balloon overtubes. Spiral overtubes are designed with a helical structure, facilitating easier passage of endoscopes and instruments through the gastrointestinal tract while minimizing trauma. In contrast, balloon overtubes feature an inflatable balloon that helps dilate strictures or blockages during procedures, providing greater stability and support. Both types enhance the efficiency and safety of endoscopic interventions, catering to various patient needs and procedural requirements in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
