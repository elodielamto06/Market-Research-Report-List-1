<p><strong>The Large Format Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LED Backlit</li><li>CCFL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large format displays market is primarily divided into two types: LED backlit and CCFL. LED backlit displays utilize light-emitting diodes to provide illumination, offering superior brightness, energy efficiency, and thinner profiles, making them ideal for vibrant signage and advertising. In contrast, CCFL (cold cathode fluorescent lamp) displays rely on fluorescent tubes for backlighting, resulting in larger, bulkier designs with lower energy efficiency. While CCFLs are being phased out due to advancing technology, they are still utilized in some older display models.</p></p>
