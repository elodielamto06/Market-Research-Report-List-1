<p><p>Clay Coated Kraft Paper is categorized into two main market types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided clay coated kraft paper has a coated surface on one side, making it ideal for applications requiring high-quality print results on one side, such as packaging and promotional materials. In contrast, double-sided clay coated kraft paper features a coating on both sides, providing enhanced printability and durability, suitable for brochures, catalogs, and applications where both sides are visible and need to be aesthetically appealing.</p></p>
