<p><strong>The Elliptical Cross Trainer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-drive Ellipticals</li><li>Rear-drive Ellipticals</li><li>Center-drive Ellipticals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elliptical cross trainer market comprises three main types: front-drive, rear-drive, and center-drive ellipticals. Front-drive ellipticals feature the flywheel located at the front, allowing for a more pronounced forward motion, which mimics outdoor running. Rear-drive ellipticals position the flywheel at the back, offering a natural, fluid stride. Center-drive ellipticals have a compact design with the flywheel at the center, providing a balanced feel and minimizing the machine's footprint, catering to various user preferences and space requirements.</p></p>
