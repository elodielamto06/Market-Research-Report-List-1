<p><p>The Spring Dampers market includes various types of springs designed for specific applications. Compression springs absorb energy when compressed, making them ideal for shock absorption. Force springs are used where constant force is needed. Tension springs offer resistance when stretched, suitable for applications requiring pulling force. Radial springs provide support in rotational systems, while garter springs are used to create seals or maintain pressure in confined spaces. Each type plays a crucial role in energy management within mechanical systems.</p></p>
