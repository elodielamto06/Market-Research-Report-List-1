<p><strong>The Surgical Drainage Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Initiative</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical drainage equipment market can be categorized into initiative and passive types. Initiative drainage systems actively facilitate fluid removal through mechanisms like suction, optimizing drainage efficiency and minimizing complications. Examples include vacuum-assisted systems. Conversely, passive drainage systems rely on gravity or natural flow, allowing fluids to drain without external aid, such as simple tubing or rigid drains. Each type serves specific clinical needs, with initiative systems often preferred in complex surgeries, while passive systems are suitable for less invasive procedures.</p></p>
