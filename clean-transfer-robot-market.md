<p><strong>The Clean Transfer Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Transfer Robot</li><li>Vacuum Transfer Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clean Transfer Robot market consists of two primary types: Atmospheric Transfer Robots and Vacuum Transfer Robots. Atmospheric Transfer Robots operate in environments where air pressure is maintained, suitable for handling products sensitive to contamination. In contrast, Vacuum Transfer Robots utilize a vacuum system to securely manipulate items, making them ideal for transporting delicate or high-value materials in controlled, low-contamination settings. Both types are essential for industries like pharmaceuticals and electronics, where cleanliness and precision are critical.</p></p>
