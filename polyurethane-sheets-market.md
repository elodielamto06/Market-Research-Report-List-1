<p><p>The polyurethane sheets market is divided into two primary types: flexible urethane materials and rigid urethane materials. Flexible urethane materials are characterized by their elasticity and ability to absorb impacts, making them ideal for applications like cushioning, seals, and flexible parts. In contrast, rigid urethane materials possess enhanced structural integrity and thermal resistance, making them suitable for applications such as insulations, panels, and construction components. Both types cater to diverse industries including automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
